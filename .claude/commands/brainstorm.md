# /brainstorm — Automation & Augmentation Opportunities

You are helping a founder identify what to automate or augment in their business using AI.

---

## Before You Start

Read context files:
- `context/workspace.md`
- `context/overview.md`

Read the wiki:
- `wiki/index.md` — scan the full catalog
- Drill into key articles: priorities, business model, tech stack, founder profile, any existing connections

If the wiki has no articles, stop and tell the founder to set up ARC first.

Then check for supporting artifacts:
- wiki articles with `type: exploration` for prior research
- recent files in `imports/`

Do not repeat ideas already well-captured in exploration articles unless the founder asks to revisit.

---

## How to Brainstorm

Analyze the founder's business using the wiki and generate concrete, actionable suggestions.

### What to look for

- **Repetitive tasks** the founder mentioned (check founder profile, priorities)
- **Pain points and bottlenecks** from the priorities article
- **Tool integrations** that could be connected (from tech stack)
- **Information flows** that are currently manual
- **Communication tasks** (emails, follow-ups, briefings)
- **Research and analysis** that could be accelerated
- **Reporting** that is currently manual
- **Content creation** if relevant

### What NOT to suggest

- Anything requiring replacing the founder's core judgment
- Complex multi-system integrations that would take weeks
- Suggestions disconnected from their actual tools or workflow
- Generic "use AI for marketing" type suggestions — be specific

---

## Output Format

### Quick Wins (can be done in a single session)

For each (aim for 3-5):
- **What**: One-line description
- **Why**: How it connects to their specific pain points
- **How**: What it would look like in practice
- **Impact**: Time saved or quality improved

### Deeper Builds (require more setup or integration)

For each (aim for 3-5):
- **What**: One-line description
- **Why**: How it connects to their business
- **What's needed**: Tools, integrations, or setup required
- **Impact**: What changes when this is working

---

## After Presenting

> "Any of these jump out? If something interests you, I can explore it — research the best approach and spec it out."

If they pick one, transition to `/explore` with that idea.

If nothing resonates:

> "What did I miss? Is there something you do every day or week that feels like it shouldn't take as long as it does?"

### File useful ideas back

If the brainstorm produced valuable analysis about the business (e.g., you identified a pattern across multiple wiki articles), consider creating a `wiki/connections/` article capturing that insight.
